How to nominate

To get started with nominations please follow the steps below

  1. Ensure you have been added as a user by your organisation co-ordinator.
  2. Log in with your username and passowrd. You can also log in with your Diksha ID.
  3. Access your "My Programs" tab and view the available programs.
